ISHANYA is a two day design exhibition organised by the Department of Design (DOD), IIT Guwahati, to be held from 26th to 27th April 2018. It is a festival where design students showcase their work. The speakers include eminent designers from a wide range of specialisations. The exhibits showcase work across the domains of Interaction, Visual, Animation, Games, Systems, Product and Automobile.

India is a country rich in customs, rituals and practices which form a colourful picture portraying India’s uniqueness - Unity in diversity. Situated in the north eastern part of India, DoD is the gathering ground where people from varied backgrounds congregate together to learn and explore design.
 
Ishanya, which signifies the North East corner, is considered to be holy and sacred by Indian scriptures. This exhibition is an array of the academic works of the Master and Bachelor Thesis Projects of the year 2018 at DOD, IIT Guwahati. This exhibition is an expression of the student’s latent talent in varied fields. It’s the collective synergy of the journey undertaken together by the students to develop their ideas and spread the message of meaningful design.
